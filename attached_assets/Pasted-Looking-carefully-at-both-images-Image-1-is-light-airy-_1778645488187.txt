Looking carefully at both images — Image 1 is light, airy, editorial (white/cream background, real interior photos, clean layout) while Image 2 is the current dark olive Process section. The goal is to bring that light editorial energy into the Process section.

Design Direction: Light Editorial Process Section
Core Shift
Dark olive → Warm Alabaster (#F5F2ED) as the base. Keep the gold accents but let them breathe against cream rather than glow against dark.

Layout Concept — "Split Story" Style (inspired by Image 1's photo + text split)
Instead of 4 floating circles in a row, do this:
Left side (40%): A tall stacked column of real interior photos — like Image 1's overlapping/offset photo collage. Two photos slightly offset, one overlapping the other. This grounds the section in real work rather than abstract steps.
Right side (60%): The numbered process steps listed vertically, like a refined editorial list — not a horizontal timeline.

Typography Treatment

Eyebrow: HOW WE WORK — Montserrat, 9px, letter-spacing 0.3em, Bronze (#6D5A41)
Main heading: Our Process — Playfair Display, 48px, Olive (#21291a), left-aligned (not centered)
Step numbers: 01, 02, 03 — Playfair italic, very large (80px), Gold at 15% opacity as a ghost watermark behind each step title
Step titles: Playfair Display, 24px, Olive
Step descriptions: Lora, 15px, Bronze, line-height 1.8
Thin 1px Gold horizontal divider between each step


What to Borrow Directly from Image 1
The overlapping photo collage: Image 1 has two photos — one slightly behind the other, offset diagonally. Do the same on the left side of the Process section:

Top photo: a living room or bedroom shot, slightly tilted or just offset upward
Bottom photo: a kitchen or workspace shot, overlapping the first at the corner
Add a thin Gold border frame (1px) around each photo
Small Gold square accent dot in the corner where they overlap

The "Explore Our Services" button style from Image 1: Use the same solid dark button style for a CTA at the bottom of the right column — "Book Free Consultation" — dark olive background, alabaster text, Montserrat all-caps.
The icon style from Image 1: Each step gets a very minimal line icon to its left (just like Image 1's lightbulb/wrench icons) — but smaller, in Gold, sitting beside the step number.

Detailed Step Layout (Right Column)
