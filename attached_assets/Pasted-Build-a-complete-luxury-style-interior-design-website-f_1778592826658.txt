Build a complete, luxury-style interior design website for NIVORA Interiors, a boutique interior design studio. The website must feel calm, elegant, premium, minimal, and high-converting, not like a typical bright WordPress template. Use the attached logo as the brand reference for color direction and overall mood.

Brand Identity
NIVORA is a boutique interior design studio creating thoughtful, refined spaces that balance elegance with everyday functionality. The brand should feel like a premium design studio for high-end clients in Mumbai and Pune. The voice must be calm, confident, elegant, and not salesy.

Visual Style
Create a dark luxury website using a toned-down palette inspired by the logo:

Primary background: deep forest green / olive green.

Accent color: warm beige / muted gold.

Secondary surfaces: cream / alabaster / off-white.

Text: soft grey and charcoal for readability.

Avoid bright colors, pure black, pure white contrast, flashy gradients, or loud animations.

Keep sections spacious, refined, and minimal.

The design should feel like a boutique interior studio, not a generic agency site.

Typography
Use an elegant serif for headings and a clean sans-serif for body text.

Headings: a refined serif style, thin, spaced, editorial, premium.

Body: clean sans-serif, highly readable.

Navigation and service labels should be modern and simple.

The typography should match the luxury tone of the logo.

Website Goals
The website must:

Look premium and dark-minimal.

Generate inquiries.

Show services clearly.

Present portfolio/projects beautifully.

Tell the brand story with trust and authority.

Work beautifully on mobile.

Load fast and remain SEO-friendly.

Include smooth, subtle motion only.
